Death Wish Rafting Company

Company Purpose: to bring to pass death defying trips; Mission: To get the largest adrenaline hit for the rest of their life. Preferably one that lasts longer then the trip. Creed: tell death rapids sweep us apart

History

This company started back January 1st 2000 when the founders, Fred and George, decided that life was boring and not worth sitting around doing nothing. Fred tried a stunt with rock climbing and got what he later learned was an adrenaline rush. He was hooked. He started seeking out ways to get another rush and brought his brother George into it. They finally settled on river rapids as the perfect sport for it. Finding that they didn't want to go back to their boring desk jobs, they quit and started Death Wish Rafting Company for all adrenaline junkies and those who have death wishes.
